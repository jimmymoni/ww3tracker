# Israel-Hezbollah Conflict

## Overview
The Israel-Hezbollah Conflict represents the most dangerous front in the Middle East crisis. What began as support operations for Gaza has escalated into full-scale war between Israel and the Iranian-backed Lebanese militant group.

---

## Root Cause

### Shebaa Farms Dispute
Territorial dispute over border area claimed by Lebanon, occupied by Israel since 1967.

### 2006 War Legacy
Unfinished business from 2006 Lebanon War. Hezbollah rebuilt stronger with Iranian support.

### Iranian Armament
Hezbollah equipped with 150,000+ rockets via Iranian supply lines through Syria.

### US-Iran War Activation
Hezbollah ordered to open northern front against Israel as part of regional conflict.

---

## Key Players & Motivations

### Israel
**Motivation:** Eliminate Hezbollah threat, secure northern border
**Capabilities:** Air superiority, Iron Dome, intelligence dominance

### Hezbollah
**Motivation:** Support Iran, defend Lebanon, attack Israel
**Capabilities:** Rocket arsenal, tunnel networks, Iranian support

### Lebanon (State)
**Role:** Affected party with limited sovereignty over Hezbollah

### Iran
**Role:** Hezbollah sponsor providing weapons and funding
