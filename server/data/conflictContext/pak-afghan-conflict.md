# Pakistan-Afghanistan Border Conflict

## Overview
The Pakistan-Afghanistan Border Conflict represents the latest escalation in decades-long rivalry between nuclear-armed neighbors.

---

## Root Cause

### The Durand Line Dispute
The border drawn in 1893 has never been recognized by Afghan governments.

### Taliban 2.0 and the TTP
The Afghan Taliban harbor Tehrik-i-Taliban Pakistan (TTP) militants.

### The Drone Threat
Afghan Taliban developed drone capabilities threatening Pakistani cities.

---

## Key Players & Motivations

### Pakistan
**Motivation:** Eliminate TTP threat, secure western border
**Capabilities:** Nuclear weapons, F-16/JF-17 fighter jets

### Afghanistan (Taliban Government)
**Motivation:** Assert sovereignty, resist Pakistani dominance
**Capabilities:** 70,000+ fighters, captured US equipment

### TTP
**Motivation:** Overthrow Pakistani government
**Capabilities:** 5,000-10,000 fighters, suicide attacks

### China
**Role:** Mediator protecting CPEC investments
