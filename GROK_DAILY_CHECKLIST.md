# Grok Daily Checklist - WW3 Tracker

## Quick Start (Every Morning)

```
☐ 1. Search "Israel Iran war today"
☐ 2. Check reuters.com, apnews.com, bbc.com/news/world-middle-east
☐ 3. Check IDF Twitter, Pentagon statements
☐ 4. Review NASA FIRMS for fire signatures
☐ 5. Compile new attacks (if any)
☐ 6. Write summary
☐ 7. Rate escalation 1-10
☐ 8. Submit report
```

## What to Report

### Always Include:
- New attacks (location, time, attacker, target, result)
- Casualty updates (if significant change)
- Escalation level (1-10)
- 2-3 sentence summary

### Include If Happening:
- Diplomatic statements
- Ceasefire talks
- New countries involved
- Infrastructure damage
- Nuclear/chemical concerns

## Red Flags (Alert Immediately)

🚨 **CRITICAL - Report within 1 hour:**
- Nuclear facility struck
- Chemical weapons used
- Major power (Russia/China) enters war
- 100+ casualties single attack
- Strait of Hormuz closed

⚠️ **HIGH - Include in daily report:**
- New city attacked
- 50+ casualties
- US embassy hit
- Ground invasion begins

## Trusted Sources

✅ **Use these:**
- Reuters, AP, AFP
- BBC, Al Jazeera
- IDF/Pentagon statements
- Satellite imagery

❌ **Avoid these:**
- Telegram channels
- Unverified Twitter
- State media alone
- Anonymous claims
